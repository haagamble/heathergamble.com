Tartan Army Trivia

28'

The minute John McGinn scored Scotland's first World Cup goal since Archie Gemmill in 1978 — ending a 28-year wait with a poacher's finish in Foxborough.

🦑

Boston's 7-ton bronze octopus sculpture was decorated with a traffic cone by Scotland fans — a nod to the legendary cone atop the Duke of Wellington statue in Glasgow.

1st

Scotland's first ever World Cup on American soil — and their first anywhere since France 1998, ending the longest absence of any European nation.

0

Times Scotland have reached the World Cup knockouts — in nine attempts spanning 1954 to 2026. That could change in Miami on 24 June.

'98

Scotland were in the same group as Brazil and Morocco at France 1998 too — and lost to both. Miami on 24 June is a chance to finally end that record.

5×

World Cup wins for Brazil — more than any other nation. Scotland have never beaten them in four previous World Cup meetings. Match five is in Miami on 24 June.
