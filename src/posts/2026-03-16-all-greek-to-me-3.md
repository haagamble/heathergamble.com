A few days ago we visited **Acrocorinth**, the massive fortress rising above the ancient city of Corinth. Athens has the **Acropolis**. That got me thinking about prefixes. What does _acro_ mean? <!-- excerpt -->

While thinking about the word _acropolis_, I realized that the second part of the word comes from the Greek word **πόλη**, which means _city_.

I wondered what words I could learn from suffixes and prefixes of English words that are derived from Greek. I've been chatting with my favourite AI chatbot, and here are some examples. These should be easy to add to my working vocabulary now.
