How to Use the App

This app helps you learn Greek verbs through multiple-choice quizzes and sentence practice.

  • Quiz Questions: You'll see a Greek verb and need to choose its English meaning from the options.
  • Correct Answers: Get 3 correct in a row to level up. Wrong answers drop you a level.
  • Sentence Practice: After a correct answer, practice by saying a sentence or viewing examples.
  • Final Level: At the highest level, enjoy varied questions and keep your streak going!
  • Progress: Track your current level and correct streak.
